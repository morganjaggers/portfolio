Prepare to be Manipulated Website

I hardcoded and independently designed a digital website named, 'Prepare to be Manipulated'. The main artefact of the site being a coded chatbot (in which I named 'SocialBot') inside. The site is entirely interactive with added content throughout, including scalable vector diagrams (SVG) animations and Spark AR filters (which I created using external softwares).
The website is an interactive, digital scroll and educational site to express the perils of social media to the younger generation and in return, the user receives valuable information on how to overcome any potential issues from being online (via the chatbot). The site outlines how you are being manipulated and outputs actions to change and to be educated.
