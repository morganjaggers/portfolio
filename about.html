About Me

I am currently an IT/Digital Graduate at Rentokil Initial.

I have always had a passion for Digital and Social Media, as well as showcasing my creative side, in design and development tasks.

I am known as the big brown eyed, only child, technology lover. I always have had a camera in my hand! I love to go to the gym, paddle board, create AR filters and try new foods from different cultures.

Travelling is also a huge passion of mine, and I am very fortunate to have been able to travel as much as I already have. It has helped me to gain a better perspective of who I am, and I believe you can never travel too far.

As a keen user of many different social platforms and skilled in many different softwares and coding languages, I hope to expand my knowledge and to continue to do what I love daily.
