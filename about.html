About

Hi, I'm Morgan Jaggers!

Living 5 minutes from Gatwick Airport and 45 minutes from Central London, I am Currently a Digital Media BA Undergraduate at Roehampton University.

I have always had a passion for Digital and Social Media, as well as showcasing my creative side, in design and development tasks.

Being raised as a small town, brown haired and eyed, only child, technology lover, I always had a camera in my hand! Apart from that, I love to go to the gym, meditate and try new foods from different cultures

As a keen user of many different social platforms and skilled in many different softwares and coding languages, I hope to expand my knowledge and to continue to do what I love daily.
